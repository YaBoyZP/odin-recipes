Oven Roasted Asparagus

This roasted asparagus recipe is incredibly popular among Allrecipes community members

Ingredients

  • Bunch of asparagus
  • 3 tsp of Olive Oil
  • garlic
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. get a bowl to mix:
    • Bunch of asparagus
    • 3 tsp of Olive Oil
    • garlic
    • salt
    • pepper
  3. Bake 12-15 minutes
